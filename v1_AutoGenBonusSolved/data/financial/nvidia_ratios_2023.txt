NVIDIA Corporation (NVDA) FY2023 Financial Ratios & Balance Sheet Summary
Date: February 2023

1. Valuation Metrics:
- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio (Trailing 12 Months): 72.5x (Reflects massive future growth expectations embedded in the stock price).
- Price-to-Sales (P/S) Ratio: 35.2x
- Price-to-Book (P/B) Ratio: 45.1x

2. Profitability and Return Ratios:
- Return on Equity (ROE): 91.4%
- Return on Assets (ROA): 45.2%
- Gross Margin: 72.7%
- Operating Margin: 54.1% (Incredible operating leverage due to software-like margins on hardware sales).

3. Liquidity and Solvency:
- Current Ratio: 3.5 (Extremely highly liquid).
- Debt-to-Equity Ratio: 0.22
- Total Long-Term Debt: $9.7 Billion
- Cash and Cash Equivalents: $25.9 Billion
- Net Cash Position: $16.2 Billion

4. Risk & Competition Commentary:
The company's balance sheet is incredibly healthy. The primary growth catalysts remain the continued adoption of generative AI by hyperscalers (Microsoft, Google, AWS). A secondary risk factor is the increasing competition from custom in-house silicon developed by these same hyperscalers (e.g., Google TPU, AWS Trainium), which could pressure margins in the long term.
