Apple Inc. (AAPL) FY2023 Financial Ratios & Balance Sheet Summary
Date: October 2023

1. Valuation Metrics:
- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio (Trailing 12 Months): 29.5x
- Price-to-Sales (P/S) Ratio: 7.2x
- Enterprise Value to EBITDA (EV/EBITDA): 22.1x

2. Profitability and Return Ratios:
- Return on Equity (ROE): 171.9%. (Note: Apple's aggressively high ROE is an accounting artifact resulting from its massive share repurchase program which has driven the book value of equity very low).
- Return on Assets (ROA): 28.5%
- Gross Margin: 44.1%
- Operating Margin: 29.8%

3. Liquidity and Solvency:
- Current Ratio: 0.99 (The company operates with a slightly negative working capital cycle, leveraging its immense power over suppliers).
- Debt-to-Equity Ratio: 1.76
- Total Long-Term Debt: $95.2 Billion
- Cash and Cash Equivalents & Marketable Securities: $162.1 Billion
- Net Cash Position: $66.9 Billion

4. Capital Allocation:
- Share Repurchases (FY23): $77.5 Billion
- Dividends Paid (FY23): $15.0 Billion
